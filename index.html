About Me:


I was born in China and moved to San Francisco when I was six. I grew up in a multigenerational immigrant household of ten. I knew from a young age that education would open more opportunities for me, so I studied diligently and graduated from UC Berkeley with a major in Biology and a minor in Data Science.

I originally wanted to go into cancer research with my biology major, but after taking a few data science classes, I realized that I love coding more than I liked biology. Therefore, I started exploring the world of tech. I was inspired by projects that used technology to empower others and drive positive impact. I continue to be impressed by how empowering technology can be and I want to be a part of the tech industry to create my own impact through tech.
